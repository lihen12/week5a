Where Am I From?

Chicago

I was born in the city of Chicago on July 17th, 1994. I lived there for about 5 years before moving to Sammamish where I've lived in for the majority of my life. I love visiting Chicago regularly because my entire mother's side of the family uprooted from China and moved there.

Sammamish

I moved to Washington State when I was around 5 years old. I lived in Renton for 1 year before settling down in Sammamish to the present day. I really enjoy the weather in the Seattle Area where it's not too hot, not too cold. I for one welcome the balance of rain, sunshine, and occasional snowfall.

Hobbies

Basketball / Chicago Bulls

I love to play and watch basketball. I've been playing it for as long as I can remember. If I'm not playing videogames or hanging out with friends, you'll find me keeping up to date with the NBA or playing basketball at the IMA at UW. I'm a huge Chicago Bulls fan and get quite loud when I'm watching them. My favorite player in the NBA today is Derrick Rose

Videogames

Videogames are a big part of my life. My dad introduced me to my first videogame in NBA Hangtime when I was a kid. Ever since, my brother and I enjoy playing together or online. Some games that I enjoy are League of Legends, CS:GO, NBA2K, etc.
